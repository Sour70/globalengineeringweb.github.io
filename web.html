Placement Information

Our college has a strong track record of placing students in reputable companies, ensuring a bright future for our graduates.
Placement cell helps students to put their knowledge and abilities in the right way to fetch the best of jobs. From the student perspective, high quality placements can bring a range of benefits and positive impacts.
Since inception GLOBAL is famous for Placement record, we have best placement in central India; All eligible students are placed in various MNC’s. Last year our students got placed in reputed companies like TCS, Wipro, Infosys, Congnizent, L&T INFOTECH, Persistent, KPIT Technologies, Zycus, Zensar, Hexaware, Mphasis and many more.
The Training and Placement Office at GLOBAL a robust and transparent placement process which ensures that all students get equal chance in any placement drive according to their eligibility and skills’ expertise matching the requirements of the recruiter. To ensure that no student misses an opportunity, a detailed database of students is maintained covering their academic scores and technical expertise. Several communication methods are adopted to share the information with the students in real-time. Special training sessions are also arranged to groom the skills of the students for specific technologies as required by the companies visiting us. Thus, the placement process of GLOBAL proves to be most effective among the Top Engineering & Management Institutes in Jabalpur.
